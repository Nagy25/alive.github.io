Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Proxy Alive collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Network Information: The App scans IP addresses and ports as part of its core functionality. This data is processed locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as features used and time spent.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Link to privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the App:

  • Google Play Services

3. Foreground Service Usage

Our App uses a Foreground Service to perform IP scanning tasks even when the app is minimized. This is essential for the "Proxy Alive" functionality to ensure continuous scanning without interruption. A persistent notification is displayed while this service is active to keep you informed.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App.
  • Display advertisements (via AdMob).
  • Respond to your comments and questions.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.
