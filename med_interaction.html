Last updated: January 2026

We do not collect any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. All data entered into the app (such as drug selections) is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

All application data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage methods. You have full control over this data and can clear it at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing the app data in your device settings.

You acknowledge that the information provided by this app is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or medication. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this application.
